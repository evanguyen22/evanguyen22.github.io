I love Chili's Triple Dipper because it's the perfect way to enjoy three different appetizers at once. I enjoy the honey chipotle crispers the most.

Dubai Chocolate Covered Strawberries

I love Dubai Chocolate-covered strawberries and not just beacuse of the trend but because I love the fresh strawberries combined with the chocolate and pistachio butter.
